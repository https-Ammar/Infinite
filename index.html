Comprehensive Panel Coverages

The FAST Panel goes beyond standard allergy testing by measuring reactivity to both foods and environmental triggers for a complete picture of what may be causing your symptoms.

88 Foods Tested

From dairy, eggs, meats, gluten, legumes, and nuts to everyday staples as our panel uncovers hidden food allergies and sensitivities that may be driving inflammation, digestive issues, fatigue, and skin flare-ups.

88 Environmental Factors

Dust, pollen, mold, pet dander, and other common exposures are carefully analyzed to help you identify overlooked environmental allergies that could be impacting your health.

By combining food allergy testing and environmental sensitivity testing, the FAST Panel reveals what conventional tests often miss by giving you clear answers and a personalized path to relief.
